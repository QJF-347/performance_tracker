About Us

The Story

Hey there! I'm Octopus—yes, despite the name, I do not have eight hands (yet). I started this platform after noticing something troubling: students start off strong, but over time, their performance can take a nosedive. By the time anyone realizes, the damage is already done.

So, I thought, "What if I had an all-seeing eye?"—and since becoming Sauron wasn't an option, I built this platform instead. This platform helps students, parents, teachers, and admins track performance in real-time, offering insights before things spiral out of control.

Using AI, we don’t just track progress—we predict struggles, suggest improvements, and even give friendly nudges (read: virtual judgment stares) to keep students on track. No more last-minute panic before exams. No more wondering where things went wrong. Just smart, proactive learning support.

So here we are—a one-person, octopus-run operation, making education smarter one step at a time. And now, for those who love spoilers...
